How to use Gradelator

  • ☆ Add the number of columns you want (i.e. the number of subjects you want to calculate for) by clicking on the Enter key or the button.
  • ☆ Enter the subject name for your respective courses.
  • ☆ Select the corresponding credit units and grades score.
  • ☆ Click on Calculate to calculate your GPA and GPAX. You should now see your GPA and GPAX displayed below.
  • ☆ If you wish to undo the current calculation, click on the Clear button.
  • ☆ After calculation, a pop up will appear.
  • ☆ If you want to calculate your GPA/GPAX for another semester, simply click on Yes, else click on No to display your average score to end up with a 1st, 2nd class honor.
